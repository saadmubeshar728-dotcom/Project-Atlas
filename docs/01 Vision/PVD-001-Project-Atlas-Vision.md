# 1. Product Name

**Codename:** Project Atlas

**Meaning**

Atlas represents a single place where every business opportunity is
collected, organized, and transformed into growth.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 2. Product Motto

> **Every Lead Has a Destination.**

Every imported lead should move through a meaningful lifecycle:

``` text
Discovered
    ↓
Imported
    ↓
Qualified
    ↓
Contacted
    ↓
Negotiation
    ↓
Won
    ↓
Project
```

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 3. Vision Statement

Project Atlas is an offline-first Lead Intelligence Platform for
freelancers, agencies, and small businesses. It helps users capture
public business information from supported sources, organize it, score
it using deterministic rules, and convert opportunities into projects.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 4. Mission Statement

To eliminate repetitive lead management tasks by creating a platform
where discovering, organizing, qualifying, and converting leads becomes
fast, consistent, and enjoyable while keeping users in control of their
data.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 5. Core Principles

1.  Every Lead Has a Destination.

7.  Build the Core Once. Expand Forever.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 6. Problem Statement

Agencies and freelancers collect leads from multiple sources, resulting
in scattered information across browser tabs, spreadsheets, notes, and
messaging apps. This creates duplicate work, missed opportunities, and
poor visibility into the sales pipeline.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 7. Solution

Atlas provides a unified workflow:

1.  Detect supported pages.
2.  Ask the user before importing.
3.  Normalize and validate data.
4.  Score and categorize leads.
5.  Store them locally.
6.  Guide leads through a structured lifecycle until they become
    projects.
