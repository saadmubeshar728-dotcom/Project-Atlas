# 1. Product Vision

Project Atlas is an offline-first Lead Intelligence Platform that helps freelancers, agencies, and businesses capture, organize, score, and manage business leads. Its purpose is to transform scattered business opportunities into a structured sales pipeline.

**Core Philosophy:** Every Lead Has a Destination.

---

# 2. Product Overview

Atlas consists of two primary components:

- Atlas Browser Extension
- Atlas Desktop Application

The extension captures supported business information after user confirmation, while the desktop application validates, scores, stores, and manages those leads locally.
